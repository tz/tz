Gifts

I usually have most of what I need and much of what I want. If you'd like to give me a gift, my favorite things to recieve are consumables I can eat or drink, invitations/tickets to local events I'm able to attends, donations in my name to organizations like 5th Square, Neighborhood Bike Works, and the EFF, stickers, books, and money. I am less enthusiastic about clothing, household items, art, gadgets, gear, furniture, and so on. Here is a short list of things I am almost always happy to receive:

  • cheese
  • peaty whiskey
  • interesting stickers (2-3 copies even better)
  • flavored nuts
  • dried figs
  • ice cream, espcially rum raisin
  • a pint at the local bar
  • a fruit or vegetable I've never had before
  • baijiu
  • soju
