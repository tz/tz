Ted Zellers

I design software, advocate for complete streets, play Eurogames obsessively, and enjoy live electronic music. I believe that will power is always in short supply; therefore we should design our houses, streets, and transit systems so that the easiest choices are also healthy and environmental. I'm currently designing a Passive House triplex that I will live in. Get in touch at mail@thiswebsite.com .
